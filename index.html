About u-insure

U-insure is Uganda's first digitized insurance platform that supports all the insurance companies enrolled on the platform.

It is easy to use and customized to the different insurance products of the various companies.

It uniquely identifies each client enabling payments over various payment channels and offering real-time reconciliation.


DISCLAIMER

All the data collected and submitted on this application is totally depedent on the entries done by the agents of the insurance companies that carry out the data collection and on the clients that provide this information. The validation of this information is managed and handled by the insurance company that is collecting this data.
